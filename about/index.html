Our origins

Fitset, founded in 2015, is an Edmonton-based, and Edmonton-run wellness startup created by Tim Gourlay, a University of Alberta Graduate and MBA. With a passion for living a healthy and active lifestyle, Tim developed Fitset after experiencing the cost barrier and inconvenience of holding multiple studio memberships. Fitset was founded on the belief that life is better when you are physically acitve.

Whether it's a spin class, a challenging boulder climb, or a restorative yoga class, Fitset connects members to unique fitness experiences at an affordable cost. With a hard-working customer service team, and a growing community of users, Fitset is the most impactful way for Edmontonians to train and enhance their physical and mental being.
