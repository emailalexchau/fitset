Common trial questions

Your free trial starts immediately after you sign-up.

Your membership gets you all the features and benefits of a regular membership. You are given 20 complimentary credits to use in your free two week trial, enough to book at least 2 classes.

Credits are used to book classes at your favourite studios. The average class cost in Edmonton is about 9 credits, but you'll find great classes and even open gym access for as low as 6 credits. More popular studio locations and popular class times cost a little bit more, while less popular class times cost a little bit less.

Yes, at the end of your 14-day trial, your membership renews into our lowest cost membership at $45 plus gst and gets you 25 monthly credits. Packages with more class credits are available to upgrade to at any time.

For our regular memberships, up to 15 unused credits rollover into the next cycle so you can use them up when your schedule allows. For our trial members, your 20 complimentary credits unfortunately do not rollover.

To cancel your membership, login to your profile, visit the billing page and simply hold or cancel your membership. If done before your trial ends, you won't be charged.
